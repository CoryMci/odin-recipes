Fried Rice

Rice mixed and fried with a meat, eggs, and vegetables.

For this recipe, we will be using chicken and eggs as our protein. We will need to cook the rice a day prior to allow it to dry out. It tastes good as fuck.

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Cooked Chicken
  • Egg
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic
  • Peas
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Put pan on medium/medium high
  2. Cook Peas, Garlic, Carrots
  3. Mix eggs
  4. Chuck in Chicken and season
  5. put the rice in
  6. soy sauce that mfer
  7. eat that shit up
